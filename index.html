Linear Algebra

A journey from the humble arrow to the 3D rendering pipeline

This course tells the story of linear algebra as it's actually used. We start with something as ordinary as an arrow drawn in the plane, and end up at the math that powers video games and 3D rendering engines. Along the way: linear transformations, systems of equations, Gaussian elimination, determinants, inverses, rank — each idea introduced first by its motivation and geometric intuition, then by its formula.

Sources and inspiration

This course leans heavily on two landmark references in the teaching of linear algebra. At the end of each section, a Further reading block points to the matching episode or lecture whenever one exists.

  • Essence of Linear Algebra — 3blue1brown. A 16-video series of short, beautifully animated explanations that lead with geometric intuition. YouTube playlist ↗
